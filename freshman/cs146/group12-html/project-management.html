The Waterfall Method

This method is a pretty cool technique used to help enhance the design of a software when developing it. It was given its name due to the nature of how it flows


Requirements and analysis

All possible system requirements are gathered during this part of the process

Design

This part creates the architecture of the project, which is pretty important

Implementation

This is where you get to sit in front of a computer and design your masterpiece! Basically just coding the project.

Testing

The fun part of any project is when you get to test out your code and discover any whacky bugs.

Deployment

Here you get to show off your product to clients and consumers so that they will buy it.

Maintenance

Basically this is just providing support to customers if anything malfunctions.

Requirements Document

It is also pretty important to have a requirements document when approaching any project. We had one for this site!

Generally all requirement documents are concise, however there are 4 main things to address

The document should: describe the problem, show a basic solution to the problem, express any constraints to the solution, and determine how success will be measured.
